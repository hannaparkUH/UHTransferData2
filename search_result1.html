UH System Course Transfer Database

Courses transferable as BIOC 241 at University of Hawai’i, Mānoa

Transferring from Hawai’i Community College


You have 2 options:

  • You may take BIOC 241: Fund of Bioch at HCC to receive credit for BIOC 241 at UHM
  • You may take BIOCH 241: Fund of Biochem at HCC to receive credit for BIOC 241 at UHM

Transferring from Honolulu Community College


You have 3 options:

  • You may take BIOC 141: Fundamentals of Biochemistry at Honolulu to receive credit for BIOC 241 at UHM
  • You may take BIOC 241: Fund of Bioch at Honolulu to receive credit for BIOC 241 at UHM
  • You may take BIOCH 241: Fund of Bioch at Honolulu to receive credit for BIOC 241 at UHM
